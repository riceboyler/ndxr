Welcome to ndxr!

ndxr is the FamilySearch Indexing Challenge site, where you can track your FamilySearch Indexing and challenge your friends and family to top your scores.

Earn achievements and badges!

As you index more and more records, you can earn achievements and special badges you can use on your website, in forms, on Facebook/Twitter/Pinterest, etc.

Have fun while helping millions of people

Don't know what this FamilySearch Indexing thing is?Visit the FamilySearch Indexing page for more information. Then come back here and login to start tracking your results.
